Who We Are

Mainframers India is a vibrant community of mainframe professionals, developers, system programmers, and enthusiasts across India.

We believe that mainframes are not legacy systems of the past, but powerful platforms driving critical business operations today and tomorrow. Our community brings together experienced professionals and curious newcomers to share knowledge, discuss best practices, and advance mainframe technology in the Indian IT landscape.

Whether you work with z/OS, manage CICS transactions, develop COBOL or PL1 applications, or are just curious about these powerful systems, you'll find a welcoming community here.
